# Video Content for AI vs Real Quiz

## Required Videos (6 total)

### Real Videos (3)
Place these in the `/static/videos/` folder:

1. **real-1.mp4** - Street Scene
   - Authentic footage of urban life
   - Suggested: City street with pedestrians, cars, natural movement
   
2. **real-2.mp4** - Nature Clip  
   - Documentary-style nature footage
   - Suggested: Forest, ocean waves, or wildlife

3. **real-3.mp4** - Human Movement
   - People in natural motion
   - Suggested: Sports, dancing, or everyday activities

### AI Videos (3)
Place these in the `/static/videos/` folder:

1. **ai-1.mp4** - Generated Motion
   - AI-synthesized scene (e.g., Runway ML, Pika Labs, or Sora)
   - Look for: Subtle physics issues, morphing objects

2. **ai-2.mp4** - Digital Environment
   - AI-generated landscape or scene
   - Look for: Inconsistent lighting, object permanence issues

3. **ai-3.mp4** - Synthetic Action  
   - AI-created movement clip
   - Look for: Unnatural joint movements, flickering details

## Tips for Finding Content

### Real Videos
- Pexels.com (free stock video)
- Unsplash (video section)
- Archive.org (public domain)

### AI Videos
- Runway ML gallery
- Pika Labs examples
- OpenAI Sora demos (if available)
- Synthesia AI demos

## Video Specifications
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec preferred)
- Duration: 5-15 seconds recommended
- Resolution: 720p or 1080p
- File size: Keep under 10MB each for fast loading
